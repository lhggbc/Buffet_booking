Our Story

Every Flavor Tells a Story

Gourmet Gala, established in 1950 in Hong Kong, is a renowned western food buffet celebrated for its fine dining experience. Known for hosting themed dinners, it offers a diverse array of global flavors alongside classic dishes. Resilient through economic changes, it adopted a farm-to-table approach in the 1980s, sourcing fresh local ingredients. Gourmet Gala blends exquisite recipes with modern twists, inviting guests to savor rich history and delightful flavors at every themed event, creating unforgettable dining experiences.
